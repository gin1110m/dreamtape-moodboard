BRAND PERSONALITY & ARCHETYPE
DREAMTAPE™ · VISUAL SYSTEM 1.0[ WHO THE BRAND IS ]FIG.A · RATIO 80/20
■ 80% Magician■ 20% Everyman

Magician, with an everyman's hands.

Magician (80%). dreamtape builds a world and lets you walk in, never explaining how the trick is done. The work is the film; the music is its soundtrack. It doesn't sell or shout — it shows you a landscape and lets you decide to stay.

Everyman (20%). Two makers standing beside the listener, not above. Spoken in a real voice, never performed. No luxury posturing, no hype. The distance is artistic, never social.

The promise stays quiet and absolute: deliver a dream to those who can no longer hold one.

This guide sets the visual direction for every dreamtape asset. Creative should not deviate.
