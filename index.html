BRAND PERSONALITY & ARCHETYPE
DREAMTAPE™ · VISUAL SYSTEM 1.0[ WHO THE BRAND IS ]FIG.A · RATIO 80/20
■ 80% Magician■ 20% Everyman

Magician, with an everyman's hands.

Magician (80%). dreamtape builds a world and lets you walk in, never explaining how the trick is done. The work is the film; the music is its soundtrack. It shows you a landscape and lets you decide to stay.

Everyman (20%). Two makers standing beside the listener, not above. A real voice, never performed. No luxury posturing, no hype.

The promise stays quiet and absolute: deliver a dream to those who can no longer hold one.

Creative should not deviate from these guidelines.
