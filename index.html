BRAND PERSONALITY & ARCHETYPE
Art Direction Guide[ WHO THE BRAND IS ]The archetype drives every other decision in this guide.
■ 80% Magician■ 20% Everyman

Magician, with an everyman's hands.

Magician (80%). dreamtape builds a world and lets you walk in, never explaining how the trick is done. The work is the film; the music is its soundtrack. It doesn't sell or shout — it shows you a landscape and lets you decide to stay.

Everyman (20%). Two makers standing beside the listener, not above. Spoken in a real voice, never performed. No luxury posturing, no hype. The distance is artistic, never social.

The promise stays quiet and absolute: deliver a dream to those who can no longer hold one. If you know, you know.

This guide sets the visual direction for every dreamtape asset. The creative should not deviate from these guidelines.

OVERALL BRAND MOODBOARD
Art Direction Guide[ THE BRAND AT A GLANCE ]The high-level feeling every asset should ladder up to.
The calm before a track drops.

dreamtape lives in the quiet confidence of someone with nothing to prove. The light is always warm and going down; the world is always a little bit dreamed. It feels like a private moment from a film you half-remember, scored by music that hasn't reached the chorus yet. The work is the cinema. The sound is the soundtrack.

13
TEXTURES
Art Direction Guide[ MAKE IT RECORDED, NOT RENDERED ]Drag every clean image back through analog texture.
Grain first. Add 35mm film grain over the photo layer, then desaturate before blending.
Halation. Let highlights bloom — a soft warm glow around the brightest edges.
analog grain · halation · leak
Light leak. Warm leak at one edge, set the layer to "Screen", low opacity.
Pull it down. Mute saturation, lift the blacks slightly — sun-faded, never crushed.
