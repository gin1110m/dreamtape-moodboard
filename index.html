02 · Personality & Archetype

Magician,
with an everyman's hands.

80% · Magician
20% · Everyman

dreamtape is a Magician at its core. It builds a world and lets you walk in, without ever explaining how the trick is done. The work is the film; the music is its soundtrack. It doesn't argue, sell, or shout — it shows you a landscape and lets you decide to stay.

The spice is the Everyman. Two directors, Satoru and Ginpei, standing beside the listener, not above them. Spoken in a real voice, never a performed one. No luxury posturing, no hype, no self-inflation. The distance is artistic, never social.

The promise is quiet and absolute: deliver a dream to those who can no longer hold one — without judging how big or small that dream is. If you know, you know.

03 · Visual Brand Pillars

The five ingredients.

Lay the ingredients on the table first, then cook only with these. Positive constraints — every frame, sleeve and clip is built from this shelf.

I

Cinematic LA Vintage

Golden-hour Los Angeles as a film set. Anamorphic width, palm silhouettes, hazy sun, motel neon, the calm of dusk. The backdrop the soundtrack scores.

II

Tape, as a feeling

Not the cassette — the warmth left on it. Hiss, grain, the bloom of light on worn film. A dream caught, then let to fade a little. Suggested, never spelled out.

III

Vintage · Organic · Dry

Analog over digital. Film grain, dust in the light, sun-bleached color, no gloss. Warm, hand-made, slightly faded — the texture of memory.

IV

Dreamscape

The landscape that appears the instant you hear the sound. Open roads, desert horizons, hazy oceans — wide, quiet, and just unreal enough to be a dream.

V

Presence, dissolved

The makers are felt, not shown — a figure melting into light, a room just left, a curtain still moving. Atmosphere stands in for the face. The magician never steps out from behind the trick.

RULE

One world, no exceptions.

If a frame can't be made from these five ingredients, it doesn't belong to dreamtape. The outline stays sharp. Always lowercase. Audio is never separated from image.

04 · Overall Brand Mood

The calm before a track drops.

dreamtape lives in the quiet confidence of someone who has nothing to prove. It is freedom expressed as image — not loud, just undeniably sure of itself. The light is always warm and going down. The world is always a little bit dreamed. You feel like you've caught a private moment from a film you half-remember, scored by music that hasn't reached the chorus yet. The work is the cinema. The sound is the soundtrack.

08 · Analog · Vintage / Organic / Dry

09 · Motion & Video Style

Footage that remembers.

Short form
Super-8 & handheld. In-the-moment, slightly shaky, real. Drives, soundchecks, the road home.
Long form
Anamorphic film stills set in motion. Slow pushes, no cuts on the beat. The visualizer is a short film.
Treatment
Date-stamp, gate weave, halation, tape hiss made visible. 24fps feel.
Rule
Audio and image ship as one package — never a track without its picture.
